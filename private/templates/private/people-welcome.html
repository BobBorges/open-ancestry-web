People Views

Welcome to the people section!

Select a person on the sidebar (to the left) to view and edit their information, incl. events, epochs, and relationships.

People and sources are at the center of data structure, so for instance — a person must exist in the database before you can create Events, Epochs and/or Research Items related to that person; two people must already exist in the database before you can define a relationship between them; a source must exist in the database before it can be assigned as an attribute of a Person, Event, Epoch, or Relationship.

Add a Person

When a person is not already defined, you can add them to the data base by clicking 'Add a Person' below.
